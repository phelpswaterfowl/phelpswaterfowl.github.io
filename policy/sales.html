Sales Policy

Duckings / Eggs

I will sell pet quality ducklings starting in the spring. No shipping and don’t ask. No fertile egg sales.

Breeder Birds

I offer breeder quality adults starting in May of each year. Birds can be delivered to any of the shows that I attend. Pricing depends on quality of birds. All birds must be paid in full prior to delivery. These are birds capable of producing top offspring.

Show Quality Birds

Everyone’s definition of show birds is different. I will not sell a bird that is labeled “show quality”. My breeder birds are priced by quality. And depending on the show, they may do very well for you.
